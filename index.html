Tips to Select a Ring

Diamond Color

The color of a diamond greatly affects its beauty. Subtle variations in hue result from natural elements, creating unique sparkle and brilliance.

Diamond Purity

Purity determines clarity and brilliance. Fewer inclusions allow light to travel freely, reflecting dazzling colors and enhancing value.

Types of Faceting

Diamonds can be cut in classic, step, or mixed styles. Each faceting method enhances light reflection differently, creating sparkle and perfect brilliance.
